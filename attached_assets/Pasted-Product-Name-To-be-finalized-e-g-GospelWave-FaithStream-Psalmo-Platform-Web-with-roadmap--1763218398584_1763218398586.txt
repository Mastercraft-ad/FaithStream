Product Name: (To be finalized — e.g., GospelWave, FaithStream, Psalmo)
Platform: Web (with roadmap for Mobile App: iOS & Android)
Purpose:

To create a Christian-centered streaming platform where users can access Gospel music, sermons, lyrics, podcasts, devotionals, and Bible readings in one clean, distraction-free environment.

The platform will unify spiritual content consumption—similar to Spotify or YouVersion but exclusively Gospel, faith-based, and scripture-focused.

2. Vision Statement

To build the world’s most trusted Christian media platform, empowering believers globally to stream, read, pray, worship, learn, and grow their faith through a modern digital experience.

3. Mission Objectives

Create a safe, pure Christian space for audio and spiritual content.

Enable churches, gospel artists, and pastors to upload and share content.

Deliver a modern, seamless, beautiful interface for worship and learning.

Promote daily spiritual growth with personalized experiences.

Build a global community of believers.
